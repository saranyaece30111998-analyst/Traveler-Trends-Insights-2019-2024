# 🌍 Traveller Insights Trends Patterns (2019–2024)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes worldwide tourism trends between **2019–2024**, focusing on:
- Tourist arrivals by country
- Annual trip spending
- Seasonal travel preferences
- Transport satisfaction levels
- Traveler segmentation (solo, family, group, business)
- Purpose of visit (leisure, medical, cultural, etc.)

The insights are presented through an **interactive dashboard** to support tourism boards, agencies, and policymakers in making **data-driven decisions**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
1. Identify **Top 10 Countries** by tourist arrivals  
2. Track **Total Trip Spend by Year**  
3. Analyze **Trips by Season**  
4. Compare **Satisfaction by Transport Mode**  
5. Segment travelers by **Trip Type**  
6. Study **Traveler Distribution by Purpose of Visit**

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques
- **Excel** for preprocessing, cleaning, and modelling
- Functions: `IF`, `AVERAGE`, `AVERAGEIF`, `ISBLANK`
- **Data Analysis Toolpak** for mean, median, mode
- **PivotTables** for deeper insights
- Relationships between tables for integrated analysis

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights
### Key Findings:
- **France** leads in tourist arrivals  
- Spending dropped in **2020** (pandemic) but recovered steadily till 2024  
- **Summer** is the busiest travel season  
- **Air travel** shows highest satisfaction  
- **Group tours** dominate traveler type  
- **Leisure/Tourism** is the most common travel purpose  

---

## 🔎 Analytical Insights
- **Descriptive:** France highest arrivals, summer peak, air travel satisfaction  
- **Diagnostic:** 2020 drop due to restrictions, recovery post-2021  
- **Predictive:** Egypt & Thailand emerging, spending to rise beyond 2024  
- **Prescriptive:** Focus campaigns on France, promote off-season packages, enhance transport services
